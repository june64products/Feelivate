# Design System: Emotional Time Travel (Next-Level Edition)

## Visual Direction: Aurora Kinetic

A fluid, high-energy aesthetic characterized by complex mesh gradients, grain overlays, and motion-heavy typography.

### 🎨 Color Palette (Aurora Mesh)
- **Primary:** Electric Blue (`#22D3EE`)
- **Secondary:** Royal Purple (`#8B5CF6`)
- **Accent:** Pink Rose (`#F472B6`)
- **Background:** Deep Cosmic (`#020617`)
- **Surface:** Ultra-Glass (`rgba(15, 23, 42, 0.4)`) - High blur, thin borders.

### 🔠 Typography (Kinetic)
- **Headings:** `Syncopate` (Bold/Extrabold) - Kinetic reveals, staggered character animations.
- **Body:** `Space Mono` - Clean, data-driven feel.

### ✨ Next-Level Motion Graphics
1. **Aurora Mesh:** 3-4 moving radial gradients in the background with a 15-20s loop.
2. **Noise/Grain Overlay:** A subtle fixed noise SVG to provide a premium "paper/film" texture.
3. **Kinetic Text:** Scrolling character reveals using `framer-motion` and `split-type` concepts.
4. **Parallax Depth:** Elements moving at different speeds on scroll to create a 3D sense.

### 🏗️ Structure
- **Hero:** Massive, screen-filling kinetic header.
- **Agents:** Horizontal "Slide" or "Bento" cards with hover-scaling.
- **Stats:** Floating "bubbles" or a tight Bento grid.
- **Testimonials:** Large-card focus with intense glassmorphism.
